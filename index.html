About the Hackathon

The Climate Action Hackathon 2025: Urban Heat & Flood Resilience is a collaborative innovation event that challenges participants to create digital solutions addressing the climate risks cities face — especially rising heat and flash floods.

  • Develop prototypes, apps, and dashboards that help cool urban areas and manage flood risks effectively.
  • Work in multidisciplinary teams of students, developers, designers, and community innovators.
  • Access workshops, mentoring sessions, and climate datasets to transform ideas into impactful digital tools.
  • Encourage innovation and sustainability through real-world, data-driven urban resilience projects.

SDG 13 – Climate Action

The Climate Action Hackathon empowers innovators to create practical, data-driven solutions that make cities more resilient to heat and floods. By combining technology, creativity, and community insight, participants will design smart systems that cool urban areas, manage stormwater effectively, and strengthen early warning networks.

Tracks & Challenges

  • Smart Cooling: Design urban cooling systems using green mapping and smart shading.
  • Flood Resilience: Create real-time dashboards and early-warning tools.
  • Green Infrastructure: Map eco-friendly areas, trees, and sustainable drainage systems.
  • Community Engagement: Build apps that empower citizens to participate in climate action.
